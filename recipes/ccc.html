Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a popular type of cookie that is made with a dough containing chocolate chips or chunks. They are known for their soft and chewy texture, with a sweet and rich flavor from the chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Butter: Softened unsalted butter is typically used as the fat in the cookie dough.
  • Sugar: A combination of granulated sugar and brown sugar is commonly used to provide sweetness and moisture.
  • Eggs: They help bind the dough together and add moisture.
  • Vanilla extract: Adds flavor to the cookies.
  • All-purpose flour: Provides structure and texture to the cookies.
  • Baking soda: Helps the cookies rise and gives them a slightly crisp exterior.
  • Salt: Enhances the flavor of the cookies.
  • Chocolate chips or chunks: The star ingredient that gives the cookies their chocolatey goodness. You can use milk, dark, or semi-sweet chocolate, depending on your preference.

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set the oven to the specified temperature in the recipe (usually around 350°F or 180°C).
  2. Cream the butter and sugars: In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract: Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, until well combined.
  4. Mix the dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Add the chocolate chips: Fold in the chocolate chips or chunks into the cookie dough until evenly distributed.
  6. Scoop and shape the cookies: Use a cookie scoop or spoon to portion out the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Leave some space between each cookie to allow for spreading.
  7. Bake the cookies: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for the specified time in the recipe, usually around 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Cool and enjoy: Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy the delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!
