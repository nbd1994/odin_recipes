Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a popular type of pizza that features a savory combination of cheese, tomato sauce, and slices of pepperoni, which is a type of cured and seasoned Italian sausage.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough: You can use store-bought or homemade pizza dough.
  • Tomato sauce: A flavorful tomato sauce serves as the base for the pizza.
  • Mozzarella cheese: Shredded mozzarella cheese is commonly used for its melty and gooey texture.
  • Pepperoni slices: Thinly sliced pepperoni is the key ingredient that gives the pizza its distinct flavor.
  • Olive oil: Used for brushing the pizza crust and adding flavor.
  • Optional toppings: You can add additional toppings like sliced onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives, depending on your preference.

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set the oven to the specified temperature in the recipe (usually around 450°F or 230°C).
  2. Roll out the dough: On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Prepare the sauce: Spread a thin and even layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Add the cheese: Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire pizza.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni: Place the pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese, ensuring they are spread out to cover the pizza.
  6. Optional toppings: If desired, add any additional toppings of your choice, such as sliced onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives.
  7. Brush the crust: Lightly brush the edges of the pizza crust with olive oil. This will help give the crust a golden and crispy texture.
  8. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for the specified time in the recipe, usually around 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Cool and serve: Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade pepperoni pizza!
